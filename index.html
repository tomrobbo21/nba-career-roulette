Spin Boosts

How Spin Boosts Work

Spin Boosts are a strategic helper you can use during your career.

Use before a spinSpend 1 Spin Boost before eligible spins to increase the chance of a better outcome.
Re-spin after a resultSpend 1 Spin Boost after eligible results to replay that same wheel with normal odds.
Earn moreChampionships, MVPs, scoring titles and DPOY awards can add extra Spin Boosts.
